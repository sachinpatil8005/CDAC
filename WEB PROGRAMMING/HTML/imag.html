Must visit

Anjuna Beach

Goa is a land of sun, sand and sea along with being home to several forts and churches. It is also the party capital of India and one of the best destinations to enjoy sunrise and sunset.The sun looks stunning when it spreads its orange and red hue in the sky and the entire backdrop becomes gorgeous when sea reflects the breath taking scene. Sunset in Goa is the most wonderful and memorable view for which most of the itinerants visit this destination. Here are the top 10 spectacular points to enjoy sunset in Goa. Anjuna Beach is widely popular to enjoy the stunning sunset views. Visit this beautiful place before sunset, and immerse yourself in the gorgeous surroundings where beach shacks plays loud music. Enjoy the changing color of the sky from blue to yellowish and then to orange. Sunset at Anjuna Beach is a must watch thing in Goa.
